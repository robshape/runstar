RUNSTAR

Privacy Policy

Information Collection

Runstar collects anonymous usage statistics that are related to app usage. This includes, but is not limited to, app launches, games started, and games completed. Collected information is transferred securely to a third-party analytics service and aggregated.

Runstar reads your location to show your position on a map, to generate gameplay elements, and to record the distance that you have moved, once you explicitly allow it using the built-in permission mechanism in iOS. When you complete a game your location information for that game is stored locally on your device by Runstar and is secured using the built-in Data Protection mechanism in iOS. Location information is stored to help you explore where you have exercised. Runstar never transmits location information to any place outside your device.

Runstar writes the distance that you have moved and the time you have exercised to Apple Health, once you explicitly allow it using the built-in permission mechanism in iOS. A copy of that information is also stored locally on your device by Runstar and is secured using the built-in Data Protection mechanism in iOS. This information is stored to help you explore how much you have exercised. Runstar never reads any information from Apple Health and never transmits health information to any place outside your device.

Information Usage

Shapeless AB may use collected information to improve Runstar and to improve customer support. Collected information is not shared with any other third parties expect for in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property. In the future, we may sell to, buy, merge with, or partner with other businesses. In such transactions, collected information may be among the transferred assets.

Consent

By using Runstar you consent to this Privacy Policy. You may opt-out of anonymous usage statistics by uninstalling Runstar from your device.
